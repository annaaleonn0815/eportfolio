Collaborative Discussion 1 Summary In Collaborative Discussion 1, I was required to select a coding weakness identified by OWASP and create a flowchart of the steps that may have led to the weakness occurring, as well as identify the appropriate UML diagram. I chose Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), a security vulnerability that allows attackers to inject malicious code into a legitimate website, enabling them to steal sensitive information or perform other malicious actions. My post emphasizes the significance of implementing proper input validation and output encoding techniques to prevent XSS attacks.Among the various UML models, I identified the Use-Case Diagram to be the most relevant due to its ability to visually represent the proposed software's design. This discussion post on XXS and the proposed software design has prompted me to realize that coding goes beyond the technical aspect of writing efficient and functional programs. It has emphasized the importance of considering the context in which my software will be used and the potential vulnerabilities it may face. Feedback from peers, My peer responses
